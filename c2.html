Ceramic Tiles

Ceramic tiles are one of the most popular flooring materials used in homes. They are made from natural clay and fired at high temperatures, making them durable, affordable, and easy to maintain. Ceramic tiles are available in various colors, patterns, and finishes, making them suitable for kitchens, bathrooms, bedrooms, and living rooms.

  • ✔ Water-resistant
  • ✔ Easy to clean
  • ✔ Available in many colors and designs
  • ✔ Affordable price
